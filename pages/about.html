About Me

I am a creative individual, originally from Germany but have since settled and made a home in England. My main drive in life is to gain more knowledge about programming and advance in my career as a Web Developer without losing my creative outlook. I will always look for new ways to improve myself and my work.

For as long as I can remember, art and creation has been my passion. What better way was there than combining the two and becoming a full-stack web developer? I now get to create webpages and use my creativity for a job I love.

My skills currently include HTML/CSS/Sass, JavaScript, as well as PHP and Laravel. But I am constantly looking for a new challenge and to learn as much as possible.
