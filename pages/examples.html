JavaScript Pagination

After setting up variables, including one to say how many items there should be on the page. Next, I created a for-loop to determine the number of pages needed. I then set the first pagination link to be active and loop though the pagination links when the eventlistener detects a click-event. The active class is being removed from any of the others to prevent two page numbers being active. The last loop goes through the projects and makes sure the correct projects are applied.
